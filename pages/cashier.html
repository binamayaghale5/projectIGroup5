Cashier

Everest Bank, Bhaktapur

Job Type: Full-time

Experience: Minimum 1 year of banking or cashier experience

Qualification: Bachelor's degree in Commerce or related field

Salary: NPR 25,000 – 40,000 per month

Job Description:

  • Handle cash transactions efficiently and accurately
  • Maintain cash drawer and balance cash at end of day
  • Provide excellent customer service at the counter
  • Process deposits, withdrawals, and payments
  • Follow bank policies and regulatory requirements

Skills Required:

  • Strong numerical and cash handling skills
  • Good communication and interpersonal abilities
  • Attention to detail and ability to work under pressure

How to Apply:

Send your updated CV to hr@everestbank.com.np or apply directly via Job Sahayatri portal.
