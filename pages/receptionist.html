Receptionist

Central Plaza, Biratnagar

Job Type: Full-time

Experience: Minimum 1 year of relevant experience

Qualification: Minimum Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or equivalent

Salary: NPR 25,000 – 35,000 per month

Job Description:

  • Manage front desk reception and greet visitors warmly
  • Handle incoming calls and emails professionally
  • Schedule appointments and coordinate meeting rooms
  • Maintain office security and visitor logs
  • Assist with administrative tasks as required

Skills Required:

  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Proficiency with MS Office and basic office equipment
  • Organized, punctual, and customer-service oriented
